Dumplings

Incredients

Flour
Baking Powder
White Sugar
Salt
Butter or Margarine
Milk

Methode

Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Overhead shot of flour and dry ingredients being whisked together for dumplings Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. Stir in milk and mix until a batter forms that is thick enough to be scooped with a spoon. Allow batter to rest for 3 to 5 minutes. High angle shot of mixing bowl with butter being cut into flour with a dough cutter High angle shot of a dough being mixed with a spatula for homemade dumplings Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes. Serve.
